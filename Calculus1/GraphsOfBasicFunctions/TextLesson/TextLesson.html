1.1 - Graphs of Basic Functions

There are 13 different basic graphs that you will use in calculus. Every graph that your class will expect you to know will come from these 13 graphs, and some techniques that involve manipulating them. If you forget what any of these basic graphs look like, you can plot a couple of easy points and come up with them on your own.
