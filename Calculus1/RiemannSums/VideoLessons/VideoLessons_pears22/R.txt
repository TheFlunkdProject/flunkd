<BR>There is another formula for left endpoints, but both are fine. It is $$A=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}f(x_i)\Delta x$$$$x_i=a+(i-1)\Delta x$$$$\Delta x=\frac{b-a}{n}$$$n$ is the number of rectangles
<BR>$a$ is the first $x$ value
<BR>$b$ is the last $x$ value
