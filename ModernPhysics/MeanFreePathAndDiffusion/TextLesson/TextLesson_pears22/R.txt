<BR>$v_{rms}=\sqrt{\frac{3kT}{m}}$  the rms average speed of a random walker
<BR>
<BR>Collision time $\tau$ is given in $v_{rms}=\frac{\lambda}{\tau}$ so the time to take N steps is $t=N\tau$
<BR>
<BR>So combining our equations $D_{rms}=\sqrt{N}\lambda=\sqrt{\frac{v_{rms}t}{\lambda}}\lambda=\sqrt{\lambda v_{rms}t}$ and $D_{rms}\approx \frac{1}{\sqrt{n\sigma}}(\frac{3kT}{m})^{1/4}\sqrt{t}$ -very useful formula
